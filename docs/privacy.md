# Privacy Policy

**My Prompter** — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 23, 2026*

## Overview

My Prompter is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Speech Recognition

All speech recognition is performed **on-device** using Apple's built-in Speech framework. No audio data is recorded, stored, or sent to any external server. Audio is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

## Data Storage

Your prompts are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your prompts may sync across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is managed entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

We do not have access to your prompts or any data stored in your iCloud account.

## Analytics and Tracking

My Prompter does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, device information, or any other telemetry.

## Third-Party Services

My Prompter does not use any third-party services. The only network communication is iCloud sync, which is handled entirely by Apple.

## Children's Privacy

My Prompter does not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with the new date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/bogdanripa/my-prompter/issues) on GitHub.
