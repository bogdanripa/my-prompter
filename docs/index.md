# My Prompter

**Your voice-powered teleprompter.**

Speak and the words follow. My Prompter listens to your voice and scrolls your text automatically. No foot pedals, no remote controls, no scrolling by hand.

## How It Works

1. Paste or type your text
2. Hit play and start speaking
3. My Prompter highlights each word as you say it

Pause and it waits. Speed up and it follows. Skip a paragraph and it finds you.

## Use It For

- Conference talks and keynotes
- Pitch rehearsals and investor meetings
- Wedding speeches and toasts
- Video scripts and content creation
- Karaoke and sing-alongs

## Features

- **Voice-activated auto-scroll** — no buttons, no pedals
- **On-device processing** — no internet required, fully private
- **Pace feedback** — set a target time and the app tells you if you're ahead or behind
- **Works in tough conditions** — noisy rooms, accents, imperfect pronunciation
- **Multiple prompts** — manage all your scripts in one place
- **iCloud sync** — access your prompts across devices
- **Mirror mode** — for glass teleprompter setups
- **iPhone and iPad** — universal app

## Support

For questions, feedback, or bug reports, please [open an issue](https://github.com/bogdanripa/prompter/issues) on GitHub.

## Privacy

My Prompter processes all speech recognition on-device. No audio data is sent to any server. Your prompts sync via iCloud only if you have iCloud enabled on your device.
