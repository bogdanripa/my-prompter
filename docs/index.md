# My Prompter

**Your voice-powered teleprompter.**

Speak and the words follow. My Prompter listens to your voice and scrolls your text automatically. No foot pedals, no remote controls, no scrolling by hand.

## How It Works

### Full Scripts
Paste or type your script, hit play, and start speaking. My Prompter highlights each word as you say it. Pause and it waits. Speed up and it follows. Skip a paragraph and it finds you.

### Bullet Points
Write your talking points as a bulleted list. My Prompter shows them as cards and checks them off as you cover each point. Speak freely in your own words — no memorizing required.

The app auto-detects which format you're using. No settings to change, no modes to pick.

## Use It For

- Conference talks and keynotes
- Pitch rehearsals and investor meetings
- Wedding speeches and toasts
- Video scripts and content creation
- Karaoke and sing-alongs

## Features

- **Voice-activated auto-scroll** — no buttons, no pedals
- **Script and bullet point modes** — auto-detected from your content
- **Extract key points** — turn any script into bullet points with one tap
- **Pace feedback** — set a target time and the highlight color guides your pace
- **On-device processing** — no internet required, fully private
- **Works in tough conditions** — noisy rooms, accents, imperfect pronunciation
- **Multiple prompts** — manage all your scripts and notes in one place
- **iCloud sync** — access your prompts across devices
- **Mirror mode** — for glass teleprompter setups
- **iPhone and iPad** — universal app

## Privacy

My Prompter processes all speech recognition on-device. No audio data is sent to any server. Your prompts sync via iCloud only if you have iCloud enabled on your device. [Full privacy policy](privacy).
